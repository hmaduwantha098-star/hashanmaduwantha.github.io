Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Often called the "Eighth Wonder of the World," Sigiriya is an ancient rock fortress rising 200 meters above the surrounding plains. Built in the 5th century by King Kasyapa, it features:

  • World-famous frescoes of celestial maidens
  • Mirror Wall with ancient graffiti
  • Lion's Paw entrance
  • Extensive water gardens
  • 360-degree panoramic views

Best time to visit: December to April (early morning recommended)

Ella & Tea Country

Nestled in the central highlands, Ella offers cool climate, stunning views, and the famous train journey from Ella to Kandy.

Top Attractions:

  • Nine Arch Bridge - Iconic railway bridge
  • Little Adam's Peak - Easy hike with rewarding views
  • Ella Rock - More challenging hike
  • Ravana Falls - Beautiful waterfall
  • Tea factory tours - Learn about Ceylon tea production

Cultural Triangle

The heart of Sri Lanka's ancient civilization, featuring three UNESCO World Heritage sites within a triangular area.

Key Sites:

Anuradhapura
Ancient capital with sacred Bodhi tree and massive stupas
Polonnaruwa
Medieval capital with well-preserved ruins and statues
Dambulla Cave Temple
Five caves with Buddha statues and murals
Mihintale
Birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka

Beautiful Beaches

Sri Lanka's coastline offers diverse beach experiences from surfing hotspots to tranquil bays.

Popular Beaches:

  • Mirissa: Whale watching and beautiful sunsets
  • Unawatuna: Crescent-shaped bay with calm waters
  • Hikkaduwa: Coral reefs and marine life
  • Arugam Bay: World-class surfing destination
  • Tangalle: Secluded and peaceful

Wildlife & National Parks

Sri Lanka is a biodiversity hotspot with numerous national parks and wildlife reserves.

Top Parks:

  • Yala National Park: Best for leopard sightings
  • Udawalawe National Park: Elephant gatherings
  • Wilpattu National Park: Largest park with natural lakes
  • Minneriya National Park: "The Gathering" of elephants
  • Sinharaja Forest Reserve: UNESCO rainforest with endemic species

Hill Country

The cool central highlands offer scenic train rides, waterfalls, and colonial charm.

  • Nuwara Eliya: "Little England" with colonial architecture
  • Kandy: Cultural capital with Temple of the Tooth
  • Horton Plains: Hike to World's End viewpoint
  • Adam's Peak: Sacred pilgrimage mountain
