About ExploreTour

Who We Are

ExploreTour is a locally-owned tourism company founded in 2010 by a team of passionate Sri Lankan travel experts. We combine deep local knowledge with international standards of service to create authentic, memorable travel experiences.

Our Mission

To showcase the true beauty of Sri Lanka while promoting sustainable tourism that benefits local communities and preserves our cultural and natural heritage for future generations.

Your Safety & Comfort

Certified Guides

All our guides are government-licensed with first-aid training and extensive local knowledge.

Verified Partners

We work only with registered hotels, transport providers, and activity operators who meet our safety standards.

24/7 Support

Our local team is available round-the-clock to assist you throughout your journey.

Sustainable Travel

We follow eco-friendly practices and support community-based tourism initiatives.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: Genuine cultural experiences over tourist traps
  • Sustainability: Minimizing environmental impact
  • Community: Supporting local businesses and communities
  • Excellence: High-quality service and attention to detail
  • Flexibility: Customizable itineraries to suit your preferences
